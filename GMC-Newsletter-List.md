## GMC-Wide Publications

### *The Long Trail News* *(1922–present)*

- **Published by:** GMC Headquarters  
- **Frequency:** Quarterly  
- **History:**  
  - 1910–1913: Club notes in *The Vermonter*  
  - December 1922 – December 1925: Published as *Green Mountain News* (6 issues)  
  - October 1928–present: *The Long Trail News*  
- **Archive:** [gmcmontpelier.org/Archive\_Publications/LTN\_Archives](https://gmcmontpelier.org/Archive_Publications/LTN_Archives/index.htm) — 405 issues, 1922–2023  
- **Current issues:** [greenmountainclub.org/long-trail-news](https://www.greenmountainclub.org/long-trail-news/)  
- **VHS holdings:** Complete file (Doc 241; open stacks call no. 796.51 G824L)

---

### *Long Trail Guidebook* *(1917–present)*

- **Published by:** GMC Headquarters  
- **Editions:** 29 editions through 2025  
- **Historical archive:** Editions 1–23 (1917–1985) at [gmcmontpelier.org/Archive\_Publications/LongTrailGuides](https://gmcmontpelier.org/Archive_Publications/LongTrailGuides/index.htm)

---

### *Day Hiker's Guide to Vermont* *(1978–2011)*

- **Published by:** GMC Headquarters  
- **Editions:** 6 editions (1978 through 2011\)  
- **VHS holdings:** Partial set (MSA 834\)
