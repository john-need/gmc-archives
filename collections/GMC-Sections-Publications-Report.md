## Part 1 — Current Sections (14 Active)

### 1. Bennington Section
- **Location:** Bennington area
- **Trail maintenance:** Long Trail, Harmon Hill to Glastenbury Mountain
- **Newsletter:** *The Stepping Stone* — published quarterly (spring/summer, fall, winter). Color PDF editions back to 2023 available for free download; earlier issues are online as well.
- **Website:** [benningtongmc.org](https://www.benningtongmc.org/)
- **Newsletter archive:** [benningtongmc.org/newsletters](https://www.benningtongmc.org/newsletters)
- **Recent issue (Spring/Summer 2026):** [PDF](https://www.benningtongmc.org/uploads/1/2/9/9/129930871/newsletterspring2026.pdf)

### 2. Brattleboro Section
*(formerly the Windham County Section, 1947–c.1952)*
- **Location:** Brattleboro area
- **Trail maintenance:** Long Trail, Winhall River to VT 11/30
- **Newsletter:** No named newsletter found on current website. The section communicates via an email list and Facebook. VHS archives hold one newsletter from 1981.
- **Historical note:** Originally chartered in 1947 as the *Windham County Section*; renamed Brattleboro Section shortly thereafter. A 1952 motion to disband was defeated.
- **Website:** [brattleborogmc.org](https://www.brattleborogmc.org/)
- **Historical documents on website:**
  - [1947 Section Creation (as Windham County Section)](https://www.brattleborogmc.org/images/Historical_Documents/1947_05_May.pdf)
  - [1947 Long Trail News mention](https://www.brattleborogmc.org/images/Historical_Documents/1947_11_Nov.pdf)
  - [1952 motion to disband](https://www.brattleborogmc.org/images/Historical_Documents/1952_02_Feb.pdf)

### 3. Bread Loaf Section
- **Location:** Middlebury / Addison County area
- **Trail maintenance:** Long Trail, Sucker Brook Shelter to Emily Proctor Shelter
- **Newsletter:** Seasonal newsletter (no formal title found); distributed by link to Google Drive PDF. Recent issues available to members.
- **Recent issue (Summer 2026):** [Google Drive link on homepage](https://www.gmcbreadloaf.org/Home)
- **Website:** [gmcbreadloaf.org](https://www.gmcbreadloaf.org/)
- **VHS archives:** Newsletters 1977–82, 1992–93 (Doc 184:11)

---

### 4. Burlington Section
- **Location:** Burlington area
- **Trail maintenance:** Long Trail, Winooski River Footbridge to Smugglers' Notch
- **Newsletter:** *RidgeLines* — published continuously since May 1971. The most extensive section newsletter archive, with 211+ issues digitized and available as PDFs on the section website.
- **Website:** [gmcburlington.org](https://gmcburlington.org/)
- **Newsletter archive:** [gmcburlington.org/ridgelines/](https://gmcburlington.org/ridgelines/) — issues from May 1971 to Summer 2026
- **VHS archives:** Newsletters 1964–74 (Doc 184:16), 1975–82 & 1993– (Doc 184:17)

---

### 5. Connecticut Section
- **Location:** Central Connecticut
- **Trail maintenance:** Long Trail, Glastenbury Mountain to Stratton-Arlington Road
- **Newsletter:** *Trail Talk* — published quarterly (winter, spring, summer, fall). Archive available from 1999 to Spring 2023 as PDFs on the section website. Note: publication appears to have ceased or moved online after Spring 2023.
- **Website:** [conngmc.com](https://conngmc.com/)
- **Newsletter archive:** [conngmc.com/newsletters/](https://conngmc.com/index.php/newsletters/) — PDFs 1999–2023
- **VHS archives:** Newsletters 1969–75 (Doc 184:21), 1976–81 (Doc 184:22)

### 6. Killington Section
- **Location:** Rutland area
- **Trail maintenance:** Long Trail, VT 140 to Maine Junction (north of Route 4)
- **Newsletter:** *Smoke & Blazes* — published continuously since 1948, making it one of the longest-running GMC section newsletters. Current issues available on the section website; archive on Internet Archive.
- **Website:** [gmckillington.org](https://gmckillington.org/)
- **Current issue (Spring 2026):** [gmckillington.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/Spring-2026-S-and-B-revised.pdf](https://gmckillington.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/Spring-2026-S-and-B-revised.pdf)
- **Historical archive (Internet Archive):** [archive.org/details/SmokeAndBlazes_201605](https://archive.org/details/SmokeAndBlazes_201605) — large PDF covering 1948–2016 (donated by Bob Perkins to Rutland Historical Society)
- **Supplement 2013–2020:** [archive.org/details/smokeandblazes201319](https://archive.org/details/smokeandblazes201319)
- **VHS archives:** Smoke & Blazes newsletters 1948–present (Doc 301:3–12)

### 9. Montpelier Section
- **Location:** Montpelier area
- **Trail maintenance:** Long Trail, Camel's Hump to Winooski River Footbridge, and Smugglers' Notch to Chilcoot Pass
- **Newsletter:** *Trail Talk* — published quarterly since at least 1956. Full archive available on the section website.
- **Website:** [gmcmontpelier.org](https://gmcmontpelier.org/)
- **Newsletter archive:** [gmcmontpelier.org/archives/TrailTalk/index.htm](https://gmcmontpelier.org/archives/TrailTalk/index.htm) — issues from 1956 to present
- **Historical publications:** Section also hosts an extensive archive of *Long Trail News* issues and *Long Trail Guidebooks* going back to 1917: [gmcmontpelier.org/Archive_Publications/](https://gmcmontpelier.org/Archive_Publications/index.htm)
- **VHS archives:** Newsletters 1956–74 (Doc 185:5.1), 1975–92 (Doc 185:5.2), 1993–2002 (Doc 185:5.3), 2003– (Doc 185:5.4). Also: *A Capital Section: The History of the Green Mountain Club's Montpelier Section, 1955–2005* by Reidun Dahle Nuquist, 2007 (Doc 185:6b).

## Part 2 — GMC-Wide Publications

### The Long Trail News (GMC Headquarters)
The official quarterly publication of the Green Mountain Club, published since 1922.

- **1910–1913:** Club notes published sporadically in *The Vermonter*
- **December 1922 – December 1925:** Published as *Green Mountain News* (6 issues)
- **October 1928 – present:** *The Long Trail News*

Full digitized archive available at [gmcmontpelier.org/Archive_Publications/LTN_Archives/](https://gmcmontpelier.org/Archive_Publications/LTN_Archives/index.htm) — 405 issues, 1922–2023.

Current issues available at [greenmountainclub.org/long-trail-news/](https://www.greenmountainclub.org/long-trail-news/)

VHS holds a complete file (Doc 241; open stacks call no. 796.51 G824L).

### Long Trail Guidebooks
Published since 1917. 29 editions through 2025. Full archive of historical editions (editions 1–23, 1917–1985) available at [gmcmontpelier.org/Archive_Publications/LongTrailGuides/](https://gmcmontpelier.org/Archive_Publications/LongTrailGuides/index.htm).

### Day Hiker's Guide to Vermont
Published by GMC in editions from 1978 through 2011 (6th edition). Partial set at VHS (MSA 834).
